About Susan Kare

Susan Kare is a pioneering graphic designer best known for her work at Apple, where she designed many of the original Macintosh icons. Her work has had a lasting impact on user interface design.

Notable Works

Susan Kare's designs, including the "Happy Mac" and the "Trash Can" icons, became some of the most recognizable visual symbols in computing history.

Impact on Design

"Good design isn’t about what medium you’re working in. It’s about thinking hard about what you want to do and what you have to work with before you start." – Susan Kare

Her contributions set the standard for digital design and continue to influence modern user interfaces.
